About

I've spent the last 8 years helping companies ship products that people actually use — from Square's restaurant POS reaching 40,000 locations, to Stripe's dispute management tools handling $2B+ in annual volume, to Acme's dashboard redesign that moved DAU by 34%.

I care equally about craft and outcomes. Beautiful products that don't move metrics aren't finished. Metrics-moving products that feel broken aren't either. The best work is when neither is compromised.

What I'm looking for

A senior IC or lead role at a B2B SaaS company where design has genuine leverage. I do my best work when I'm close to the engineering team and the customer problem simultaneously. Remote-friendly or NYC-based.

Happy to talk about: design systems, complex information architecture, how to run a design critique that people don't dread, or why constraints are usually a feature.
