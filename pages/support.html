The Mission

Who FOLIO is for.

  • 🎓
    Students and new graduates — Building a professional presence before you have a traditional resume. FOLIO captures your projects, courses, and skills from day one.
  • 🔍
    The unemployed and job seekers — People who need tools most are the ones who can least afford them. AI-powered job targeting, gap analysis, and resumes — all free, always.
  • 🔄
    Career changers — A nurse moving into health tech. A teacher becoming an instructional designer. FOLIO surfaces transferable skills that traditional resumes bury.
  • 📈
    Career advancers — Know exactly what you've accomplished and what gaps stand between you and the next level — with real BLS salary data to negotiate from.
  • 🗂️
    Career record keepers — One place for every job, cert, project, and publication. Your whole career, structured, searchable, always accessible.
  • 🤝
    Mentors and talent scouts — Verify and attest skills. Find real candidates. Discover profiles with structured data — not keyword-stuffed PDFs.

The Opportunity

The resume market is $500M+ annually — and growing. FOLIO doesn't compete with resume builders. It replaces the format entirely. The moment a recruiter asks for a FOLIO handle instead of a PDF attachment — the flywheel starts.

Revenue comes from employers (structured talent discovery, ATS integration) and institutions (university cohort accounts) — never from the job seekers themselves.

Why Now

AI made resume generation a commodity. ATS software turned hiring into keyword matching. Candidates are invisible — not because they lack capability, but because the format is broken.

FOLIO arrives at the exact moment people are questioning whether the resume makes sense. GitHub replaced emailing zip files. FOLIO replaces attaching PDFs. The behavior shift is starting.

The working prototype is complete. The schema is designed. The AI layer is specified. This raise is to build, deploy, and acquire the first 10,000 users.
