The Builder
ME
@mario-espindola · Builder
Mario A. Espindola
MPA · IPMA-CP · Product Builder · Public Sector Strategist

I built FOLIO because I watched people — brilliant, capable, hardworking people — get filtered out by systems designed to process them, not see them. A nurse trying to move into health tech. A teacher who wants to become an instructional designer. A bootcamp grad who outworks everyone in the room but can't get past an ATS.

The resume is a 1950s technology. The PDF made it worse. I have a background in public administration, program management, and technology — and I built FOLIO to be the tool I wish existed when I was navigating career transitions myself.

This is free forever for individuals. The people who need it most shouldn't have to pay for it. That's not charity — it's architecture.

Beta Program
⚠️ Beta Disclaimer — Please Read

FOLIO is currently in closed beta. You are using an early version of the software that may have bugs, incomplete features, and unexpected behavior. Your participation helps make the product better for everyone.

Data: While we take your data privacy seriously, this is a beta environment. Do not store sensitive information (SSN, financial data, passwords) in your vault. Data may be reset between major versions with advance notice.

AI outputs: All AI-generated content (resumes, cover letters, gap analysis) is a starting point, not a finished product. Review everything before using it in a professional context. The AI does not invent facts — but verify outputs match your vault.

Free forever: The core product is and will always be free for individuals. Any future paid features will only add capability — they will never remove features currently free.

Open source: FOLIO is licensed under AGPL-3.0. The code is public at github.com/folio-so/folio. You can verify how your data is handled.
